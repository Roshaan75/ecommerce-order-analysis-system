Key Dataset Findings

Festival season demand can contribute up to 40% of annual revenue, while electronics show the highest return rate at 18% due to specification mismatches.

Business Impact

About 12% of customers contribute 65% of revenue, making customer segmentation and loyalty strategy essential.

Logistics Issue

Tier-2 and Tier-3 city deliveries average 2.3 days beyond the promised date, directly affecting customer satisfaction.
